Gor安装

下载地址：Github/goreplay

wget https://github.com/buger/goreplay/releases/download/v1.0-rc2/gor_1.0-RC2_x64.tar.gz

解压安装包

tar xzvf tar gor_1.0-RC2_x64.tar.gz

流量复制到文件

sudo ./gor –input-raw :80 –output-file requests_origin.gor

从文件中恢复流量的ip:port

sudo ./gor –input-file requests_origin_0.gor –output-http https://localhost:80
